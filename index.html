Plans, credits & add-ons
Users choose Basic, Pro, or Creator Ultimate when they create an account. Everything is managed in the Profile only.
Basic
$0 / month
No monthly fee. Light access with watermarks and lower resolutions. Great for trying things out.
  • • Access to Image, Music and Video labs with restrictions
  • • Watermarks always on
  • • Lower quality outputs (e.g., SD)
  • • Can buy extra credits when needed
Pro
$10 / month
Includes 2500 credits and access to all Pro-level features across Image, Music and Video.
  • • All Pro features in Image, Music and Video labs
  • • Higher quality outputs (HD, some UHD)
  • • Can add “Music Ultimate” for +$5/month (no extra credits)
  • • Short films and Studio advanced features stay locked
Creator Ultimate
$45 / month
Includes 10000 credits and full access, including Short Films and Studio tools.
  • • Everything in Pro plus Short Films
  • • Full Studio access for Music + Video editing
  • • Highest resolutions (UHD, 4K where supported)
  • • Access to all Ultimate versions across categories
Credit usage per feature:
• 1 Image (text or simple image-to-image) 5 credits
• Image-to-image with more work (advanced) 15 credits
• 1 Song / music generation 10 credits
• Text-to-video 10 credits
• YouTube reference (learning style) 10 credits
• Lip sync add-on for a video +10 credits
• Short film (multi-scene) 100 credits
