Plans, credits & add-ons
Users choose Basic, Pro, Creator, or Creator Ultimate in their profile. Add-ons and yearly billing are handled there too.
Basic
$0 / month · 50 credits
Try everything with a watermark. Good audio and video quality so your label never sits on something low.
  • • 50 credits per month included
  • • Image, Music, Video and Short Film previews
  • • MP3 audio only, always with watermark
  • • Cannot buy extra credits or remove watermark
  • • Great for testing the full workflow before upgrading
Pro
$20 / month · 2500 credits
For serious creators who want WAV export, higher resolutions and the ability to add Ultimate packs.
  • • 2500 credits per month included
  • • Clean outputs, no watermark
  • • WAV export for music and videos (audio)
  • • Higher resolutions (HD / UHD depending on tool)
  • • Can buy credit packs (100 / 200 / 300 credits)
  • • Optional add-ons: Music, Image and Video Ultimate (+$5 each)
Best value
Creator
$30 / month · 500 credits
For power users who want access to 4K, Studio workflows and deeper control, with focused monthly credits.
  • • 500 credits per month included
  • • 4K support for visuals where available
  • • WAV export and clean outputs
  • • Full access to Studio workflows for Music and Video
  • • Can buy extra credit packs
  • • Can add Music / Image / Video Ultimate for deep control
Best overall
Creator Ultimate
$45 / month · 10000 credits
Everything unlocked. Designed for full productions, short films and intense Studio use across all categories.
  • • 10000 credits per month included
  • • 4K support across visual tools where available
  • • Full Studio + Short Film workflow
  • • Stems & MIDI export for 50 credits per song
  • • All Ultimate features included (no separate add-on needed)
  • • Priority for future Sulandra AI Studio features
