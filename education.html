Sulandra International Academy (SIA)

Explore SIA – School of Arts with your AI mentor

This is where your trained AI model becomes a private tutor for pro users. SIA teaches everything about entertainment: writing lyrics, learning different music styles, scriptwriting and editing, acting and blocking, camera work, scene setup, visuals, lighting, and how to look and move like someone who belongs on TV and on stage.

Discipline
Lyrics & Song Styles
Learn how to write hooks, verses and bridges in Afrobeat, Amapiano, Gospel, R&B, Pop and more. Your AI mentor can suggest lines, improve your lyrics and match them to different rhythmic feels.
Later: guided lyric sessions
Discipline
Music & Performance
Understand tempo, groove, delivery, adlibs, stage presence and how to structure songs that work for live shows, dance challenges and streaming platforms.
Later: style breakdowns + exercises
Discipline
Scriptwriting & Editing
Get help drafting scripts, fixing dialogue, tightening scenes and pacing. Your AI can propose rewrites, remove weak scenes and suggest stronger structure for short films and series.
Later: script doctor AI
Discipline
Scene Setup & Blocking
Guidance on how to arrange actors, props and camera for each scene: where people stand, how they move, where the camera sits and how to make every frame clear and intentional.
Later: scene diagrams + prompts
Discipline
Lighting & Visual Mood
Learn lighting setups for music videos, interviews, dramatic film scenes and portraits, plus how to translate those setups into AI prompts for images and video.
Later: lighting recipes
Discipline
On-Screen Presence & Branding
Tips on body language, wardrobe, camera confidence, and how to build a consistent on-screen identity that feels professional and ready for TV or major platforms.
Later: persona & brand coaching
How SIA will work technically (high level):

• This Explore page sends your question + discipline to a backend endpoint such as /api/sia-mentor.
• The backend uses your trained SIA model plus curated knowledge sources (guides, notes, examples you trust).
• It returns structured answers: explanations, step-by-step plans, prompt suggestions, script edits, shot lists, lighting notes, etc.
• Over time, you can add your own curriculum, checklists and templates so SIA becomes the official Sulandra School of Arts.

For now, this page is the front-door and design for that experience, ready for when you connect your AI brain.
