Image Lab

Design, upload and rebuild your visuals with AI

Use Sulandra Image Lab to generate new images from text, upload a reference image for outfit changes or edits, or upload scripts and storyboards (PDFs) to turn scenes into visual frames. This is the control room – your future backend will connect to these tools.

AI Image Workflows
Phase 1 · UI only

Choose how you want to work: pure text prompts, upload images for edits, or upload documents with scenes that the AI will later use to create storyboards and visual plans.

Later, this will send your prompt and settings to your image generation model running on Runpod.

Example use cases: change dress color, adjust pose slightly, add jewelry, generate alternate outfits, different background, etc.

Later, your backend can read the script and ask your image model to generate one frame per scene or per key moment – perfect for films, music videos and social media stories.

All upload actions are placeholders for now. They do not send files anywhere yet – we will connect them to your backend API and Runpod later.
